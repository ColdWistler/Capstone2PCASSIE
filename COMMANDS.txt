=== DQN FLIGHT SIM — COMMANDS & ARCHITECTURE ===

--- Run ---
  godot --path /path/to/project

--- Auto-Save ---
  Weights, epsilon, and training stats auto-save every 50 episodes
  to user://dqn_weights.save (Godot's user data folder).

--- Auto-Load ---
  If dqn_weights.save exists at startup, it is loaded automatically
  and training resumes from where it left off.

--- Save Location ---
  Linux:   ~/.local/share/godot/app_userdata/<project>/dqn_weights.save
  Windows: %APPDATA%/Godot/app_userdata/<project>/dqn_weights.save
  macOS:   ~/Library/Application Support/Godot/app_userdata/<project>/dqn_weights.save

--- Training (console output) ---
  Episode N | reward: X.X | steps: M | eps: E.EEE | best: B.B
    N    = episode number
    X.X  = total reward this episode (target: high positive)
    M    = steps in this episode (target: near 2000)
    E.EEE = epsilon (exploration rate, decays from 1.0 to 0.01)
    B.B  = best reward seen so far

--- Episode End Conditions ---
  • Safe landing (parked):     +15 bonus, episode ends successfully
  • Crash:                     0 reward, episode ends
  • Max steps (2000):          episode times out, reward as-is

--- Takeoff (first launch only) ---
  Plane spawns on runway at (0, 2, -250), engine starts, accelerates
  to 30+ m/s, pitches up. DQN takes over above 50 m altitude.
  Subsequent respawns skip takeoff and spawn at altitude.

--- State (12 dimensions) ---
  [forward_speed/100, altitude/500, sin(pitch), cos(pitch),
   sin(roll), cos(roll), vertical_speed/50, is_stalled,
   obstacle_fwd, obstacle_left15, obstacle_right15, fuel_SoC]

--- Actions (7) ---
  0 = nothing
  1 = pitch up
  2 = pitch down
  3 = roll left
  4 = roll right
  5 = throttle up
  6 = throttle down

--- Network (Dueling DQN) ---
  Input(12) -> Hidden(128, ReLU) -> split into:
    - Value stream:   Hidden(128) -> V (scalar)
    - Advantage stream: Hidden(128) -> A (7 values)
  Q(s,a) = V(s) + A(s,a) - mean_a A(s,a)

--- Training Details ---
  • Double DQN:      online network selects best next action,
                     target network evaluates it (reduces overestimation)
  • Soft target:     Polyak averaging (tau=0.005) every step
                     instead of hard copy every N steps
  • Prioritized replay: SumTree with alpha=0.6, beta anneals 0.4->1.0
  • Gradient clip:   all gradients clipped to [-1.0, 1.0]
  • LR decay:        0.001 * 0.9995^step, floor 0.0001
  • Gamma=0.99, Batch=32, Replay=20k, Epsilon decay=0.998

--- Change Log ---
  • Hidden layer 32 -> 128
  • Dueling DQN: value + advantage streams instead of single output
  • Double DQN: target uses online-selected actions
  • Soft target update (Polyak tau=0.005) replaces hard periodic copy
  • Prioritized experience replay with SumTree
  • Gradient clipping at 1.0
  • Learning rate decay (0.9995 per step, floor 0.0001)
  • Fixed step_count double-increment bug
  • GPU acceleration removed (no improvement to model)
  • Save format v3 (incompatible with old saves)
